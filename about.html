Who I Am
THE ENGINEER
BEHIND THE WORK

I'm Arman Biraji, a third-year B.E. Civil Engineering student at Advanced College of Engineering & Management. Engineering, to me, is not just a discipline — it's a language for describing how the physical world can be made better, safer, and more enduring.

My academic journey has taken me deep into structural mechanics, hydraulic systems, geotechnical analysis, and construction management. But what excites me most is where civil engineering intersects with technology — BIM workflows, smart cities, data-driven infrastructure decisions.

Outside the classroom, I read obsessively about material science breakthroughs, sketch bridge cross-sections in spare time, and believe that the most beautiful things humans have ever built are infrastructure.

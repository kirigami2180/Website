Studying adaptive water supply infrastructure, flood-resistant urban design, and SWMM-based stormwater modelling. Deeply interested in designing cities as sponges — absorbing and reusing rainfall rather than expelling it, building resilience from the ground up.

Going Deeper
CURRENT STUDY PROJECTS
01
Academic Research
STRUCTURAL BEHAVIOUR OF UHPC BEAMS UNDER DYNAMIC LOAD
Studying the response of ultra-high-performance concrete beams to dynamic loading scenarios, with a focus on strain rate effects, energy absorption capacity, and residual strength post-impact. Comparing UHPC performance against conventional M40 concrete using ANSYS simulations.
02
Personal Project
GIS-BASED FLOOD VULNERABILITY MAPPING FOR COASTAL URBAN AREAS
Building a QGIS workflow to identify flood-prone zones in a coastal city using DEM data, NDVI, population density layers, and historical flood extents. The output aims to recommend priority zones for green infrastructure and stormwater interventions.
03
Coursework Deep Dive
BIM EXECUTION PLAN FOR A MULTI-STOREY RESIDENTIAL BUILDING
Developing a comprehensive BIM Execution Plan (BEP) for a 12-storey residential complex in Revit, incorporating architectural, structural, MEP, and civil models. Focus on Level of Development (LOD) standards, clash detection, and quantity extraction for cost estimation.
04
Self-Study
SMART STORMWATER SYSTEMS USING IoT FLOW SENSORS
Researching the integration of IoT-enabled flow sensors in urban stormwater networks to enable real-time monitoring and predictive overflow alerts. Exploring open-source platforms like OpenSensorHub and ThingSpeak for data visualisation and anomaly detection.
Knowledge Base
BOOKS & RESOURCES I RECOMMEND
📗
DESIGNING WITH NATURE
Ian McHarg
A foundational text on ecological planning — every civil engineer should understand the land before reshaping it.
📘
THE SMART ENOUGH CITY
Ben Green
A grounded critique of smart city hype that redirects focus toward genuinely human-centred urban technology.
📙
STRUCTURES: OR WHY THINGS DON'T FALL DOWN
J.E. Gordon
The best non-technical introduction to structural mechanics ever written. Changed how I think about loads and materials.
📕
SUSTAINABLE URBANISM
Douglas Farr
Explores walkable, transit-oriented, and mixed-use urban design — essential reading for sustainable infrastructure advocates.
📓
BIM HANDBOOK
Eastman, Teicholz et al.
The authoritative guide to Building Information Modelling — methodology, workflows, tools, and implementation strategies.
📒
THE DEATH AND LIFE OF GREAT AMERICAN CITIES
Jane Jacobs
A timeless argument for organic, community-driven urbanism — a counterweight to purely technical approaches to city design.
